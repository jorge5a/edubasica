"Practicas con Arduino" is an electronic book made by 4 spanish secondary 
school Technology teachers. This ibook gathers several classroom projects 
in order to teach students (15-16 years old) electronics, robotics and programming
focusing on Arduino board.
Edubasica is an Arduino shield designed to make easier the implementation of
"Practicas con Arduino" projects.
Practicas con Arduino eBook (spanish version) es free and downloable from 
this web site:
practicasconarduino.com  
and from iTunes for iPad version.
